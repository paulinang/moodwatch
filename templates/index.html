Keep a calendar… and every night when you go to bed, mark in that calendar whether the day was a one, which was as bad as the day you heard the news, or a ten… you won’t have tens for a long time but measure. Just mark it down. After two months, take out that calendar and put it on a graph. You’ll find that your down days are just as bad as the first day, but here’s what happens… they get further and further apart. That’s when you know you’re gonna make it… when you realize the measure of your progress.

Joe Biden in a discussion on grief
